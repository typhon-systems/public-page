Who We Are

Technology in the
public interest

We started Typhon with a simple conviction: that the tools available to those who protect the public should be at least as good as the tools available to those who threaten it. Six years on, that conviction drives everything we build.

Human-Led

Every Typhon system is designed to augment human judgement, not replace it. Our platforms present options and analysis. Decisions remain with trained, accountable professionals — always.

Transparent

We publish annual transparency reports, cooperate fully with parliamentary oversight, and maintain detailed audit trails across all deployments. Our clients can always explain what our systems did and why.

Resilient

Critical infrastructure demands uncompromising reliability. Typhon systems are built for adversarial conditions — redundant architecture, hardened endpoints, and 24/7 operational support.

Accountable

Every contract includes binding accountability clauses, mandatory human review protocols, and escalation pathways. We welcome scrutiny because we believe we have nothing to hide.

How It Works

Advanced analytics.
Built for the real world.

Typhon's platform combines sensor integration, large-scale data correlation, and predictive modelling — delivered through interfaces designed for operators under pressure, not laboratory conditions.

We do not use the phrase "artificial intelligence" because we believe it obscures more than it reveals. What we build is decision infrastructure: sophisticated analytical tooling that makes human experts faster, more consistent, and better informed.

TECH-01
Multi-Source Fusion
Data Integration Layer

Typhon's ingestion layer processes over 400 million discrete data points per hour across structured and unstructured sources — classified government feeds, commercial sensor networks, open-source intelligence, and aggregated behavioural data from partner organisations. All data is cryptographically isolated by jurisdiction and access tier.

TECH-02
Predictive Correlation
Analytical Engine

Our proprietary correlation engine identifies statistically significant relationships across disparate data sources in near-real time. Rather than flagging individual events, the engine surfaces patterns — building an evolving operational picture that persists across shift changes, jurisdictions, and time zones.

TECH-03
Scenario Modelling
Consequence Mapping

Given a set of current conditions, the modelling layer generates probabilistic scenario trees — presenting operators with likely trajectories, resource requirements, and intervention windows. All projections are time-stamped and logged for post-incident review and institutional learning.

TECH-04
Hardened Infrastructure
Deployment & Security

Typhon platforms are available in cloud, hybrid, and fully air-gapped configurations. Our secure enclaves meet NST Act 2013 requirements and are certified to COSMIC TOP SECRET handling standards. Penetration-tested quarterly by independent firms approved by the National Cyber Authority.

TECH-05
Audit & Oversight
Governance Framework

Every action taken within a Typhon deployment — every alert reviewed, every recommendation accepted or overridden — is logged to an immutable audit record. These records are available to oversight officers, parliamentary committees, and judicial processes in standardised, machine-readable formats.

TECH-06
Field Terminal
Edge Deployment

The KINGFISHER terminal brings the full analytical stack to forward-deployed personnel. Ruggedised to MIL-STD-810G, operating in satellite and mesh-relay modes, with four-hour autonomous battery life and biometric operator authentication. Weighs 1.4kg. Deployed across seven specialist constabulary units nationwide.

Product Systems

The full operational
platform

Six integrated systems, each designed for a specific operational context — deployable independently or as a unified estate, with full interoperability across the Typhon product family.

SYS-01 / WIDE-AREA
ARGUS
Pattern Recognition Network

Continuous, city-scale intelligence fusion. ARGUS aggregates signals across sensor arrays, communications metadata, and open data streams to surface emerging patterns before they become incidents. The backbone of the national deployment, now active in 34 metropolitan areas.

  • Real-time multi-source correlation across 400M+ daily events
  • Configurable alert thresholds by operational tier
  • Classified network integration at SC and above
  • Full audit trail with operator attribution
SYS-02 / FLAGSHIP SYSTEM
SENTINEL
Command Decision Platform

The primary interface for command-level officers. SENTINEL translates ARGUS data into a coherent operational picture, presenting scenario models, resource maps, and recommended intervention windows. Used by 34 constabularies and 3 defence commands across the Commonwealth.

  • Live scenario trees with probability weighting
  • Multi-agency coordination and tasking layer
  • Air-gapped and hybrid deployment certified
  • Shift-persistent situational awareness
SYS-03 / PERSISTENT
THORNFIELD
Long-Duration Area Monitoring

Rolling 90-day surveillance and event correlation for defined zones. THORNFIELD builds a temporal picture of a location — establishing behavioural baselines and flagging deviations that may not register as individual incidents but form a meaningful pattern over time.

  • 90-day rolling event archive with full indexing
  • Baseline deviation alerting, configurable sensitivity
  • Third-party sensor compatibility via open API
  • Jurisdiction-partitioned encrypted storage
SYS-04 / MOBILE
KINGFISHER
Tactical Field Terminal

Compact, field-deployable intelligence terminal for specialist and rapid-response units. KINGFISHER delivers the SENTINEL analytical stack to forward-operating personnel with minimal infrastructure dependency — in any environment, at any hour.

  • MIL-STD-810G hardened, 1.4kg chassis
  • Satellite and mesh-relay network modes
  • Biometric operator authentication
  • 4-hour autonomous operation, hot-swap battery
SYS-05 / STRATEGIC
ORACLE
Long-Range Forecast Platform

Probabilistic analysis for policy, procurement, and strategic planning. ORACLE synthesises geopolitical, social, and economic indicators to stress-test institutional strategy against modelled future conditions — with scenario drilling from 12 to 48 months ahead.

  • 12–48 month probabilistic projection models
  • Ministerial reporting templates (STRAP compatible)
  • Sensitivity analysis and red-team scenario mode
  • Secure inter-departmental distribution layer
SYS-06 / GOVERNANCE
CODEX
Compliance & Oversight Framework

End-to-end governance tooling for responsible deployment. CODEX provides legal compliance mapping, immutable audit logging, and parliamentary-grade reporting — ensuring that every Typhon deployment remains under meaningful human oversight at all times.

  • Automated decision audit logging — immutable record
  • NST Act 2013 statutory compliance mapping
  • Oversight officer real-time dashboard
  • Parliamentary Select Committee reporting templates

Our Commitments

Openness is not
optional for us

We operate in spaces where public trust is everything. We do not take that trust for granted, and we believe the best way to earn it is to be consistently, verifiably transparent about what our systems do and how they are governed.

Annual Transparency Report

Every year, Typhon publishes a full account of system deployments, the volume and type of alerts generated, operator override rates, and any incidents involving disputed outputs. The 2014 report is available for download. The 2015 report will be published in February 2016.

Parliamentary Oversight

Our government contracts require mandatory reporting to the relevant parliamentary committee for each jurisdiction. Our CLO, Sir Keir Starmer, appears before the Home Affairs Select Committee annually to answer questions on platform operations, data handling, and legal compliance.

Independent Audit

All Typhon deployments are subject to annual independent audit by firms approved by the National Security Technology Regulator. Audit reports are submitted to the Regulator and, where operationally permissible, made available in summary form to the relevant parliamentary committee.
